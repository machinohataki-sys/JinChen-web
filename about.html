Our Factory, Our Promise

Jinchen Crystal is a rhinestone manufacturer — we own the factory, the production lines, and the quality control process. When you work with us, there is no middleman between you and the people who make your crystals.

Zhejiang Jinchen Accessories Co., Ltd. (浙江今辰饰品有限公司) was established in September 2020 in Pujiang, Zhejiang. Our 30,000 m² modern smart manufacturing base features high-standard cleanroom workshops and an intelligent warehousing system, with an annual output exceeding 5 million pieces — a true large-scale production advantage.

Our elite team of 250 professionals includes R&D engineers, process specialists, and skilled technicians — with senior technicians accounting for 15% of our workforce, forming an integrated “industry-academia-research-application” talent matrix.

Between 2022 and 2024, we invested over ¥100 million in our “High-Tech Factory” initiative, completing full-process automation across 8 core modules: pointing, blister round grinding, vacuum coating, smart gluing, nano color plating, and more. The result: 300% efficiency increase, single-line capacity exceeding 2,000 pcs/day, labor costs reduced by 60%, and process precision reaching ±0.01mm — an industry benchmark.

Guided by our philosophy — “Quality achieves the future, integrity builds glory” — we have earned the trust of buyers across Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, India, and Southeast Asia. We are committed to long-term partnerships, continuous innovation, and delivering optimized products with responsive, efficient service.
