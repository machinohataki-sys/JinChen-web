Our Factory, Our Promise

Jinchen Crystal is a rhinestone manufacturer — we own the factory, the production lines, and the quality control process. When you work with us, there is no middleman between you and the people who make your crystals.

Founded in 2010 in Pujiang, Zhejiang — the global epicenter of crystal manufacturing — Jinchen Crystal has grown from a small workshop into a full-scale factory with multiple production lines, advanced cutting and coating equipment, and a dedicated quality control laboratory.

Every rhinestone we sell is made in our own facility. We source raw optical glass, cut it to 14 precision facets, apply vacuum-deposited coatings, inspect every batch through our three-stage QC process, and package it — all under one roof. This end-to-end control is what allows us to guarantee consistent quality and competitive wholesale pricing.

Our mission is to drive innovation and development in the accessories industry. Today, our factory produces over 5 million pieces per day across 76+ colors, serving wholesale buyers in more than 50 countries. We welcome clients to visit our factory in person — because we believe that seeing our production process is the best proof of our quality.
